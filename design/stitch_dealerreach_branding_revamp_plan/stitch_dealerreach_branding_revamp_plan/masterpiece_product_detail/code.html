The Legacy

Forged in Paris,
Destined for History

Founded in 1908 by Albert Dupuy, the atelier was born from a singular passion: to perfect the art of the vaulted oven. For over a century, we have remained a family-owned Maison, rejecting mass production in favor of meticulous hand-assembly.

Every Château range is built from start to finish by a single craftsman in our workshop outside Paris. This intimate connection between maker and machine ensures that every knob, every hinge, and every seal meets our uncompromising standards of permanence.
